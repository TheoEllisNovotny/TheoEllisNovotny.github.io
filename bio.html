Biography

Theo was born in 2004 in Newark, DE and he moved to Burlington, VT in Summer 2010. He is a graduate of Burlington High School, and spent most of his time there as a member of the theater program. From March 2021 to June 2022, BHS held classes in an old Macy's that had been badly converted into a high school. Theo is an identical twin, and his brother Miles goes to The University of the Arts in Philadelphia, PA.
